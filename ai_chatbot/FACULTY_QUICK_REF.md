# Faculty Graph Commands - Quick Reference

## 📊 Individual Student Graphs

```
"Create a graph of [student name]"
"Graph [student name]'s performance"
"Show me a chart for [student name]"
```

**Shows:** Student's grades across all your subjects
**Permission:** Only students in your classes

---

## 📈 Class Performance Graphs

```
"Which subjects have low grades?"
"What subject has low grades?"
"Graph my class performance"
"Show class averages"
```

**Shows:** Average grades by subject
**Identifies:** Subjects below 75%

---

## 📅 Class Attendance Graphs

```
"Which subject has many absences?"
"What subject has poor attendance?"
"Graph class attendance"
"Show attendance by subject"
```

**Shows:** Average attendance by subject
**Identifies:** Subjects below 75% attendance

---

## ⚠️ Risk Distribution Graphs

```
"Graph high-risk students"
"Show risk distribution"
"Visualize student risk levels"
```

**Shows:** Pie chart of risk levels
**Breakdown:** High/Medium/Low risk counts

---

## 💡 Example Conversations

### Quick Student Check
```
You: "Create a graph of John Reyes"
Bot: [Shows John's grade chart]
     "His grades range from 72% to 88%"
```

### Find Problem Areas
```
You: "Which subjects have low grades?"
Bot: [Shows class performance chart]
     "MATH 101 (68%) and PHYS 201 (71%) need attention"
```

### Attendance Analysis
```
You: "What subject has many absences?"
Bot: [Shows attendance chart]
     "MATH 101 has 62% attendance, CHEM 101 has 71%"
```

### Risk Overview
```
You: "Graph high-risk students"
Bot: [Shows pie chart]
     "8 high-risk, 12 medium-risk, 25 low-risk"
```

---

## 🔄 Follow-up Questions

After any graph, try:
- "Show me the students in [subject]"
- "Who needs attention?"
- "What about attendance?"
- "Is [student name] at risk?"

---

Every graph includes:
- 📸 **PNG image** - Professional chart

- ❓ **Suggestions** - Recommended next questions
